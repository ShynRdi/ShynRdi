## 👋 About Me

Hi, I’m **Shayan Radi** — an **AI/ML Engineer**, **Data Scientist**, and **Creative Technologist**.

I started my journey as a **Web Developer** and worked with technologies like **React.js**, but over time I moved deeper into the world of **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, **NLP**, **Speech AI**, and **LLM systems**.

Today, my focus is on building intelligent systems that connect:

- 🤖 **Artificial Intelligence**
- 🗣️ **Language and Speech**
- 🎓 **Education Technology**
- 🎵 **Music and Creative AI**
- 🎨 **Generative Art**
- ⚙️ **Production-ready AI Infrastructure**

I love projects where engineering meets creativity — especially systems that can help people learn, communicate, create, and think better.
