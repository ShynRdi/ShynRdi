## About Me

Hi, I’m **Shayan Radi**, an **AI/ML Engineer**, **Data Scientist**, and **Creative Technologist**.

I started as a **Web Developer**, working with technologies like **React.js**, and gradually moved deeper into **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, **NLP**, **Speech AI**, and **LLM-powered systems**.

My work sits at the intersection of:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Language and Speech
- Education Technology
- Music and Creative AI
- Production-ready AI Infrastructure

I enjoy building systems that are not only technically strong, but also useful, human-centered, and creative.

---

## Currently Building

- Private AI assistants using **LLMs, RAG, agents, and knowledge bases**
- Real-time **Persian ASR** pipelines with VAD, Whisper, and GPU inference
- AI-powered language learning systems with **CEFR-aware content and quiz generation**
- Local LLM infrastructure using **vLLM, Docker, FastAPI, Nginx, and Open WebUI**
- NLP analytics tools for sentiment analysis, word clouds, and educational insights
